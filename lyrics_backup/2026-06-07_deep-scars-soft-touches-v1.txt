[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers burning every time you lean in close
There is a quiet rhythm in the way your shadow flows
I leave the windows open just to catch your midnight breath
You are the sweetest secret that I hold inside my chest

[Verse 1]
The dusty roads are quiet and the porch light paints the wood
I pull you closer to me, like I always knew I would
Your fingers trace the collar of my faded denim coat
I read the silent letters of the poetry you wrote

[Pre-Chorus]
The coffee on the table turning cold inside the cup
We let the hours vanish, I can never get enough
There is a gentle current pulling heavy at my soul
I lose my sense of reason and I let you take control

[Chorus]
Oh, your touch is like a slow and steady fire in the dark
Leaving deep and beautiful soft touches on my heart
I want to memorize the way you whisper in the night
And keep you wrapped around me till the morning brings the light

[Post-Chorus]
[Ad-lib] (Hold me close)
[Echo] (Till the morning brings the light)

[Verse 2]
The shadows in the corner telling stories to the room
But all my true attention is entangled up in you
I trace the little lines across the surface of your hand
We speak a quiet language only we can understand

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
There is a lonely kind of magic in the way we intertwine
I traded all my wandering to know that you are mine
And every single heartbeat is a heavy, rhythmic beat
When I pull you right against me, making everything complete
I look into your eyes and let the fire take the lead
You awakened all the passion burning deep inside Saeed
